About QCut

Free, open-source video editor with AI superpowers.

Why We Built QCut

CapCut's basic features are increasingly behind paywalls. What started as a free editing tool has evolved into yet another subscription service. We believe video editing should be accessible to everyone.

QCut is our answer: a completely free, open-source video editor that respects your privacy and gives you professional tools without the price tag. Every feature that CapCut locks behind a subscription, we offer for free.

But we didn't stop there. By integrating with video-agent-skill, we've added access to 40+ cutting-edge AI models for content generation. Text-to-video, image generation, AI voiceovers â€” all available at transparent API costs, no hidden subscription fees.
