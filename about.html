About QCut

Free, open-source video editor with AI superpowers.

Why we built QCut

CapCut's basic features are now paywalled. What started as a free editing tool has become another subscription service.

QCut is our answer: a completely free, open-source video editor. Every feature CapCut locks behind a subscription, we offer for free.

By integrating with video-agent-skill, we've added 40+ AI models — text-to-video, image generation, AI voiceovers — all at transparent API costs.
